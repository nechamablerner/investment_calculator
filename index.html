Compound Interest Calculator

Investing isn't about luck—it’s about time and consistency. This tool strips away the guesswork by calculating the impact of compound interest, allowing you to see how small actions today build significant wealth tomorrow.

To see your money’s potential, simply input these three numbers:

  1. Initial Investment: The initial "seed" money you are investing today
  2. Interest Rate: The annual rate of return you expect to gain.
  3. Year of Retirement: How many years you will let the investment sit and grow
