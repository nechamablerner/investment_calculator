Investment calculator

Investing is often seen as a game of luck, but it’s actually a game of time and consistency. Whether you are saving for a comfortable retirement, a first home, or just building a "rainy day" fund, understanding how your money grows is the first step toward financial freedom. This investment calculator is designed to strip away the guesswork. By projecting the impact of compound interest—what Albert Einstein famously called the "eighth wonder of the world"—you can visualize exactly how small, regular contributions today can transform into significant wealth tomorrow.
